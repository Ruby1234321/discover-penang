Hotel Booking Tips

Consider these simple tips when choosing accommodation for your Penang trip.

📍

Choose the Right Location

Stay in George Town for heritage attractions or Batu Ferringhi for beaches and resorts.

📅

Book Early

Hotel rooms may fill quickly during weekends, school holidays and festive seasons.

🔍

Check the Facilities

Confirm parking, breakfast, WiFi, swimming pool and family facilities before booking.
