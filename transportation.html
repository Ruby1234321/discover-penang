Transportation Guide

Explore Penang easily using buses, ferries, ride-hailing services, rental vehicles and walking routes.

Getting Around Penang

Penang offers several convenient transportation options for visitors. Choose public transport for affordability, ride-hailing for convenience or rental vehicles for greater travel flexibility.

Public and Private Transportation

Compare the most common ways to travel around George Town and Penang Island.

🚌

Rapid Penang Bus

Rapid Penang provides affordable public bus services connecting George Town, residential areas, shopping centres and major tourist attractions.

  • Affordable transportation
  • Air-conditioned buses
  • Covers many popular locations
🚍

CAT Free Shuttle

The Central Area Transit shuttle provides free transportation around selected areas within central George Town.

  • Free shuttle service
  • Suitable for heritage sightseeing
  • Stops near popular city attractions
🚕

Grab and E-Hailing

Ride-hailing services are widely available and offer convenient door-to-door transportation around Penang.

  • Easy mobile application booking
  • Cash and cashless payment options
  • Convenient for tourists
🚖

Taxi Services

Taxis are available at airports, hotels, shopping malls and several tourist locations.

  • Available at major locations
  • Suitable for direct journeys
  • Confirm the fare before travelling
Ferry Service

Travel Across the Channel

Penang ferry services connect Butterworth on the mainland with George Town. The ferry is useful for travellers arriving by train, bus or other mainland transportation.

  • Connects Butterworth and George Town
  • Offers scenic views of the channel
  • Convenient for mainland travellers
  • Provides access to public transport connections

Vehicle Rental

Rental vehicles offer greater freedom for visitors who wish to explore beyond central George Town.

Car Rental

Renting a car is suitable for families, groups and visitors planning to explore different parts of Penang Island.

  • Suitable for longer journeys
  • Convenient for families and groups
  • Useful for visiting Balik Pulau
  • Check parking availability beforehand

Motorcycle Rental

Motorcycles can be convenient for experienced riders who prefer easier parking and flexible movement through busy areas.

  • Easier parking in some areas
  • Suitable for experienced riders
  • Always wear a safety helmet
  • Follow Malaysian traffic laws

Transportation Tips

Follow these tips for a safer and more comfortable journey around Penang.

✓

Plan Your Route

Check your destination and estimated travel time before beginning your journey.

✓

Avoid Peak Hours

Traffic may become heavier during morning and evening commuting periods.

✓

Prepare Payment

Carry some cash or suitable cashless payment methods when using public transport.

✓

Use Reliable Services

Choose recognised transport providers and confirm important journey details.

✓

Follow Safety Rules

Wear seat belts and helmets and follow all local traffic regulations.

✓

Use Maps

Online maps can help locate attractions, transport stops and alternative routes.
