Why Visit Penang?

Known as the Pearl of the Orient, Penang is a UNESCO World Heritage destination celebrated for its rich cultural heritage, world-famous street food, breathtaking landscapes, and unforgettable travel experiences.

Rich Heritage

Explore historic buildings, temples and traditional streets.

Local Cuisine

Taste Penang's famous street food, from Char Kway Teow to Assam Laksa.

Beautiful Beaches

Relax by the sea and enjoy the beautiful coastline of Batu Ferringhi.

Street Art

Discover colourful murals and creative art around George Town.

Popular Attractions
