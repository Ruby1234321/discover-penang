Annual Festivals

Explore some of the most popular celebrations experienced across Penang each year.

January / February

Chinese New Year

One of Penang's largest celebrations, featuring lion dances, lantern decorations, festive markets and family gatherings.

Date Changes Yearly

Hari Raya Aidilfitri

A meaningful celebration marking the end of Ramadan with open houses, traditional Malay dishes and cultural activities.

October / November

Deepavali

The Festival of Lights is celebrated with colourful decorations, traditional lamps, Indian food and cultural performances.

September / October

Mid-Autumn Festival

Families gather to enjoy mooncakes, lantern displays and evening celebrations inspired by Chinese traditions.

July / August

George Town Festival

An annual arts and cultural event featuring theatre, dance, music, exhibitions and creative performances.

December

Christmas

Shopping malls, hotels and churches welcome the festive season with decorations, performances and community activities.

Festival Travel Tips

Follow these simple suggestions when attending popular events in Penang.

✓

Book Early

Hotels and transport may become busy during major celebrations.

✓

Arrive Before the Event

Arriving early can help you find better viewing areas and parking.

✓

Wear Comfortable Clothing

Lightweight clothing and comfortable footwear are useful for outdoor activities.

✓

Respect Local Traditions

Follow appropriate dress codes and event instructions at cultural locations.

✓

Stay Hydrated

Carry drinking water, especially during daytime outdoor events.

✓

Check Event Details

Confirm the date, time and location before travelling to the event.
