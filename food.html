Penang Food Tips

Follow these simple tips to enjoy Penang's local food experience.

🕐

Check Opening Hours

Some hawker stalls only operate during breakfast, lunch or evening hours.

💵

Prepare Cash

Some traditional food stalls may only accept cash payments.

🌶️

Ask About Spice

Tell the vendor when you prefer less chilli or a milder flavour.
