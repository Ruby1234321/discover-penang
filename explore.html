Quick Exploring Tips

Use these simple tips to make your Penang journey more comfortable and enjoyable.

1

Start Early

Visit popular attractions in the morning to avoid larger crowds and warmer afternoon weather.

2

Wear Comfortable Shoes

George Town is best explored on foot, so comfortable footwear is highly recommended.

3

Plan Your Route

Group nearby attractions together to save travel time and enjoy more places.

4

Stay Hydrated

Penang's weather can be warm, so carry enough drinking water while exploring.

5

Respect Local Culture

Dress appropriately and follow visitor rules when entering temples and religious locations.

6

Check the Weather

Bring an umbrella and check weather conditions before planning outdoor activities.
